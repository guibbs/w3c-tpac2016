W3C TPAC 2016

W3C Technical Plenary / Advisory Committee Meetings Week

LISBON, PORTUGAL
SEPTEMBER 19 - 23, 2016

The Combined Technical Plenary / Advisory Committee Meetings Week brings together W3C Technical Groups, the Advisory Board, the TAG and the Advisory Committee for an exciting week of coordinated work.

New! As part of an experiment the W3C Community Groups will be able to hold a 2 hour meeting on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday or Friday. Outside of their Community Group meetings, non W3C-Member CG participants may attend as observers the Working and Interest groups meetings who accept observers. For further details, see Participation Policies and Recommendations Need Help? Please mail w3t-tpregister@w3.org, and someone will assist you
